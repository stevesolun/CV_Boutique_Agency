# Scoring Rubric

## Score scale
- 0 to 3: weak / non-competitive
- 4 to 6: usable but weak
- 7 to 8.4: strong but with clear issues
- 8.5 to 8.9: send-ready strong
- 9.0 to 9.4: must-call quality for the right roles
- 9.5+: exceptional and rare

## Critical blocker flags
- unsupported metrics
- inflated title/scope
- generic headline
- weak summary
- buried achievements
- bloated bullets
- hallucination risk
- excessive length
- industry mismatch — **suppressed** when `intentional_transition: true` is set in memory.json; treat as a positioning challenge instead
- role mismatch

## Pass criteria
- overall >= 8.5
- no critical blockers
- user acceptance
- clear final recommendation
